University Support Services:
- Counseling center available Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm.
- Peer mentoring program for first-year students.
- 24/7 helpline: 1800-123-456.
- Online student wellness portal.

Parent Strategies:
- Encourage open communication with your child.
- Normalize asking for help when stressed.
- Guide them to use the counseling services.
- Remind them of peer mentoring options.

FAQs:
Q: What if my child feels homesick?
A: Suggest attending peer mentoring groups and contacting the counseling center.

Q: How can I support my child during exams?
A: Encourage them to use stress-management workshops and relaxation sessions.

Q: Who can my child reach out to in emergencies?
A: They can call the 24/7 helpline at 1800-123-456.
